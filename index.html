Introducing TextPad

A simple, fast, and reliable text editor where you can easily input and edit text. Whether you're jotting down quick notes, writing code, or drafting your next big idea, TextPad provides a clean and intuitive interface. It also allows you to reset your text or undo changes easily, and you can view the stack operations side by side for a clear overview of your actions. With a focus on minimalism and speed, TextPad helps you stay focused on what matters-your words-without distractions.

About

TextPad is built using stack data structures to ensure efficient text input and editing. The stack-based architecture enables fast undo and reset operations, letting you go back or clear your changes with ease.
